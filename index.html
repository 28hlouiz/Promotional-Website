Explore Our City

Discover Dumaguete

Dumaguete is the capital, principal seaport, and largest city of the Philippine province of Negros Oriental. Nicknamed "The City of Gentle People", it is renowned for the warmth and hospitality of its locals.

The city's beautiful Rizal Boulevard, historic campanario, and reputation as a center of learning in the Visayas make it a unique and charming destination for tourists, students, and retirees alike.

Curated Experiences

Scenic Walk

Rizal Boulevard

Take a scenic stroll along the famous waterfront promenade, lined with ancient acacia trees, lamp posts, and bustling food stalls.

Historic Landmark

Campanario de Dumaguete

Built in the 1760s, this historic bell tower is the city's oldest surviving heritage landmark, originally used to warn locals of pirates.

Dive with Turtles

Apo Island Proximity

Just a short boat ride away, Dumaguete serves as the primary gateway to Apo Island, a world-renowned marine sanctuary perfect for diving.

City Plaza

Quezon Park

A lush, green historic plaza in the heart of the city, perfect for a relaxing afternoon walk near the Campanario and City Hall.

Nature Escape

Casaroro Falls

Hidden deep in the neighboring mountains, this breathtaking 100-foot waterfall is a must-visit for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Mountain Resort

Forest Camp

A lush mountain resort in neighboring Valencia featuring refreshing cold spring pools, hanging bridges, and beautiful natural scenery.

Center of Learning

Dumaguete is widely known as a "university city" because of the presence of four universities and a number of other colleges where students of the province converge to enroll for tertiary education.

The city is best known for Silliman University, the country's first Protestant and American university in Asia. The large student population adds a youthful and dynamic energy to the city's culture.

City Government & Services

Office of the Mayor

Leading the city with a commitment to progressive development, environmental sustainability, and public welfare from the historic Presidencia.

Sangguniang Panlungsod

The city's legislative branch, responsible for creating ordinances that protect and uplift the lives of all Dumagueteños.

Public Services

Providing accessible healthcare, emergency disaster response, and business support to foster a safe and thriving community.
